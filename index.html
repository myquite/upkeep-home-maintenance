How It Works

Set up once, stay organized forever. UpKeep turns home maintenance from a mental burden into an automatic routine.

1

Build Your Library

Start with common tasks like HVAC filters, gutter cleaning, and smoke detector tests. Add your own custom tasks for anything unique to your properties. Each template captures what needs doing and how often.

2

Create Maintenance Plans

Group related tasks into reusable plans. Monthly home maintenance, seasonal checkups, safety inspections ‚Äî organize however makes sense for you. Plans make it easy to apply the same routine across multiple properties.

3

Assign to Properties

Apply any plan to any property with a tap. Whether you have a single home, rental units, or a vacation cabin, each property gets its own maintenance schedule tailored to its needs.

4

Never Miss a Task

Your Today view shows exactly what needs attention. Overdue items stand out in red. Upcoming tasks keep you prepared. Complete a task and it's logged in your permanent maintenance history ‚Äî great for records and resale.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

UpKeep is designed with your privacy as a core principle. We believe your home maintenance data should belong to you and only you.

Data Storage

All data you enter in UpKeep, including properties, tasks, maintenance records, and completion history, is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

No Account Required

UpKeep works entirely offline. No account creation, email address, or sign-in is required to use the app. Your identity remains completely private.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or tracking mechanisms of any kind. We have no way to see how you use the app or what data you enter.

Notifications

UpKeep uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming and overdue maintenance tasks. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is ever sent to us or any third party.

No Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to share. Your maintenance records, property information, and task history never leave your device.

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data. Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device. There are no backups on our servers because we don't have servers storing your data.
